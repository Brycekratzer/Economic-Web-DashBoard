About This Dashboard

This economic dashboard provides a real-time overview of key economic indicators. Data is updated daily and sourced from FRED and financial APIs.

Technologies Used

  • Python (Data Processing)
  • Flask (API Backend)
  • Chart.js & D3.js (Visualizations)
  • GitHub Actions (Automated Updates)

Methodology

Learn about how the data is sourced, processed, and what each indicator means.

Market Momentum

The Market Momentum panel uses 50-day moving averages and RSI calculations to determine market trends.

  • 50-Day MA: Average closing price over the last 50 trading days
  • RSI: Relative Strength Index, measuring overbought/oversold conditions

Housing Market

Housing market health is tracked using the Case-Shiller Home Price Index and current mortgage rates.

  • Case-Shiller: Tracks changes in residential real estate prices
  • Mortgage Rates: 30-year fixed-rate mortgage average

Consumer Health

Consumer financial well-being is measured through sentiment indices and unemployment data.

  • Consumer Sentiment: University of Michigan Consumer Sentiment Index
  • Unemployment: U.S. unemployment rate from the Bureau of Labor Statistics

Data Sources

Data is sourced from:

  • Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED)
  • Yahoo Finance API
  • U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis
